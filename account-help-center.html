Frequently Asked Questions
How do I list an item for sale?
To list an item, tap the "+" button at the bottom of the screen, then follow the prompts to add photos, description, price, and pickup details. Your listing will be live immediately after submission.
How do pickup and delivery work?
Mina facilitates in-person exchanges. After a purchase is confirmed, you'll arrange a meetup location with the other party through our messaging system. We recommend meeting in public places for safety.
How do I get paid for my sales?
Payment is processed through our secure payment system. Funds are typically released to your linked payment method within 1-2 business days after the buyer confirms receipt of the item.
What if the buyer doesn't show up?
If a buyer doesn't show up for a scheduled pickup, you can mark it as a no-show in the app. After 24 hours, the hold on the buyer's payment will be released, and you're free to relist the item.
